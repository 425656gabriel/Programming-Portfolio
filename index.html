Calculator

Digipets

This is a pet simulator, made in processing. The pet has four needs, each of them go down at a different pace, if the four go completely down, you lose. The pets can be moved with "WASD", It doesnt have an end.

Link to the repo

Chess Simulator

This a chess simulator, I used Swing to make the board and the pieces, I built a method for a piece to detect the possible moves, another to detect check and checkmate, and a method for the pawn to promote when it reaches the other side of the board

Link to the repo

Rhythm Game

It's a OPP-based rythm game, I used Java to make the screens. I used Swing to crates the notes and the buttons. I used array lists for the position of the notes, used a for loop to make them go down at a continous speed and an internal clock to measure the pressition of the player
